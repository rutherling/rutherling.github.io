prevention

Home fires suck because they make your insurance go up LIKE CRAZY! You don't want to be there. Additionally, fires hurt and mess up your stuff. You might accidentally kill your family! Here are some ways that you can prevent fires in your home.

Common Causes of Residential Fires:

  • Excess lint in the dryer
  • Overlapping electronic cables
  • Open flames near window treatments
  • Unattended stoves
  • Frozen foods in deep fryers
​ ​
  • Fire blanket & extinguishers
  • Fire department contact info
  • Have a plan
