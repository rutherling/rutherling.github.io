What To Do In Case Of A House Fire

  • Response
  • Prevention

  • Get Out Then Call

    • Getting out safely is most important.

  • Having A List

    • Knowing the things you must save and where they are allows for a quick exit.

  • Where Are The Exits?

    • Being aware of all exits help eleminate getting trapped.

  • Dont Open Windows

    • Opening windows allow oxygen to feed the fire.

  • Test Doornobs

    • Doornobs can give a great idea of how much fire is in the following room. touch with caution and open slowly.

  • Meet Together

    • Establishing set meeting places will prevent confusion about who is out and who is in.

  • Don't Turn Back

    • Once your safe, avoid endangering yourself.
